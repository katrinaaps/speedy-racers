

## 🏎️ 3D Oval Racing Game

A 3rd-person racing game where your **blue car** races against a **yellow** and **red** AI car around an oval track. First to complete the laps wins!

### Core Gameplay
- **Your car (blue)** is controlled with **arrow keys** — Up to accelerate, Down to brake, Left/Right to steer
- **Two AI opponents** (yellow and red cars) that drive around the track automatically at varying speeds
- **3rd-person camera** that follows behind your car as you race

### The Track
- An **oval loop racetrack** with a clear road surface, grass/ground around it, and lane markings
- A visible **start/finish line** to count laps

### Race Mechanics
- **3-lap race** — first car to complete 3 laps wins
- Lap counter displayed on screen showing progress for all cars
- A **countdown (3-2-1-GO!)** to start the race
- **Winner announcement** when someone finishes, with an option to race again

### On-Screen HUD
- Current lap number (e.g., "Lap 2/3")
- Position indicator (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
- Simple speedometer

### Tech Approach
- Built using **React Three Fiber** for 3D graphics
- Simple box-shaped cars with the right colors (blue, yellow, red)
- Smooth camera that follows your car from behind
